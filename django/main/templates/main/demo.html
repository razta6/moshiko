Stage 2:

Here is as example for the visualization and features filter

You can choose from 3 levels of filtering:

  • None: no filter is appliad. All visualiztaion levels are showen and all features are presented sorte from high to low
  • Medium: medium level of filtering
  • Local: the most localized filtering. Only the most significant features are presented.

For best results - use the "Local" level. In case no feature was found, consider lowering the threshold.
