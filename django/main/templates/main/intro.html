INTRODUCTION

Understanding predictions

Moshiko's Eyebrows app brings an easy-to-use tool which lets you understand and visualize the facial landmarks used for the system to recognize a facial image.

Face recognition algorithms use highly complicated structures, known as Deep Neural Networks, that are being used to recognize identity's based on their facial images.

Although we know how the underlying computation process works, those architectures consist millions of parameters which make it impossible to iterpert why a certain prediction was made for a certain face.

Why do we need it?

Understanding the features that gave rise to a certain prediction can help us understand our system better.

On correct predictions, this will help us gain trust in the sysytem and justify the result, thus allow it to be part of an automated process

On incorrect predictions, this will help us gain insight on why the failure occured and help improve the algorithm itself and learn its fallbacks

Our system

Using a simple interface, we let you query the system with your own images.

To demonstrate the concept and the power of visualization - your image will be compared to our database of celebs and you will know not only who do you look like, but also why.
